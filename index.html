Ingredients

  • Instant coffee powder
  • hot water
  • Sugar
  • milk

dalgona cofee making proccess

STEP ONE: take the instant coffee powder and mix it in hot water
add 2 tablespoon of coffee powder and 3 tablespoon of sugar to one cup of hot water. NOTE: hot water must be boiling hot for best results
STEP TWO: whisk whisk whisk until the coffee turns so fluffy
using a hand held mixer whisk until the coffee becomes creamy and do not be lazy if takes time as a result it would be a creamy coffee with light brownn color
STEP THREE: serve it with a hot/cold milk
serve the coffee with hot/cold milk.as the coffe is fluffy and creamy a straw and spoon is mandate..and now stir and enjoy this lockdown coffee
